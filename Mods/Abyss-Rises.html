Abyssal Systems

Abyssal Systems are a new type of Solar System, which have completely fallen to the Abyss. These Solar Systems have lost their procedural nature, instead being completely Randomized and Unique, without any bounds imposed by the Atlas Generation system. These corrupted systems feature Abyssal Planets, Hostile Corrupted Sentinels, Varying Habitation, and more

Corrupted Planets

The Spreading Corruption of the Abyss has lead to the generation of Unprecedented and Unpredictable Planets, full of an infinite variety of colours, weirder and dynamic terrain, varying features, and much more...

Corrupted Hives

The Sentinels have failed to escape the wrath of the Abyss, with several swarms being corrupted. These newly reprogrammed sentinels have concentrated in large amounts in new Corrupted Hives. These hives offer a high risk, high reward opportunity for travellers, allowing them to find hidden and unique treasures.

Improved Creatures


Amidst the failure of the Atlas' Generation Protocol, creatures have begun showing changes across the universe. These odd characteristics have begun manifesting themselves in various ways, including Frequent Size and Characteristic Variations, alongside new and Improved Colours and Markings.

Alien Nebulae


The Stellar arrangement of Galactic nebulae have been drastically altered, allowing for dynamically generated, and infinitely varied and alien wonders visible from space.


Ambient Freighters

Freighters may now move freely in the atmosphere, as well as in orbit, bringing life to the once stationary giants. 

Steller Freighters may now be raided whilst stationary, allowing for forceful control of the vessels...

Abyss-Rises: Patch Notes

Solar Systems

  • Added a new class of Star System: Abyssal Systems.
  • Abyssal Systems have been completely overtaken by the Abyss, leading to greater dangers, and much more alien-esque generation.
  • Abyssal Systems feature completely randomized generation, leading to varying habitation, planetary traits, new Abyssal systems, in-orbit dangers and more.
  • Abyssal Systems appear on the Galactic Map as special systems.
  • Rebalanced Solar System distribution.
  • Rebalanced stellar economies. 

Corrupted Planets

  • Added a new class of Planets: Corrupted Planets.
  • Corrupted Planets have been overrun by the malicious code of the Abyss, resulting in alien, nonsensical and broken worlds.
  • Corrupted Planets can often mix traits of several planets and biome types to generate a truly unique and randomized planet.
  • Corrupted Planets are often Dynamic, changing terrain, features, atmosphere and locations as a traveller explores their surfaces.
  • Added the possibility for megalithic environments on all planets. 

Sentinels

  • Added new Corrupted Sentinel Hives.
  • These appear solely on Corrupted Planets, being overrun by Sentinel drones which fell to the Abyss.
  • Corrupted Hives allow players to gain new procedural rewards, if cleared of their protectors.
  • Re-adjusted sentinel drone spawn rates to match sentinel hostility and planetary presence.
  • Added the possibility for Walkers, Quadruped, Summoner and Heavy Attack Drone sentinels to patrol planets, according to the planetary sentinel presence.

Creatures

  • Added dynamic colour assignment for creatures.
  • Added dynamic sizing and herd variance for creatures based on their traits.
  • Allowed cross-species hybrid generation.

Stellar Nebulae

  • Stellar Nebulae have been completely redone, with new, Dynamic colour variations for normal systems, and alien colour scheme generation for Abyssal Systems. 

Freighters

  • Added ambient freighter spawns in atmosphere, as well as in orbit,
  • Moving freighters disable their hanger gates in order to defend against micrometeorites, preventing player scouting.
  • Freighters can still be interacted with as normally when in their stationary position.
  • Freighters can new be raided when stationary, allowing for forceful takeovers.
  • Due to the increased risks of raids, freighters now house large amounts of sentinels on board. 
  • If a player fails to obtain a license prior to boarding a freighter, they may be attacked, or gain a fine.
